My City by Riley Vazquez

In this world there is a variety of people with their ideas, beliefs, and cultures. No place better shows this than New York City. In New York City you can find many people who have their own experiences living in New York. For some, knowledge from elsewhere now blends to the rich environment of their New York. Each person has their own wisdom that gives New York its rich variety of culture. No two people are alike in New York yet there are many things to connect with one another. You can like the same game, song, sports team, or even train as another guy. New York is really a light that draws in people. Though when there is light, there are shadows. There is no such thing as a perfect world here. In the case of New York, with all the many ideas there are, some people may have negative ideas on people and may assume the worst in people due to stereotypes. Despite this, New York City gives a unique atmosphere for people. Some people could have just arrived there, while others could have lived in New York City for their entire life. They could work at an office building or at a restaurant. They can be in a very cultural area or a generic city environment. These different experiences are all bunched together in one city.

There are several communities in New York City, not just the literal sense with the five boroughs of the Bronx, Manhattan, Queens, Brooklyn, and Staten Island. For example there are different school communities. There are schools like New Exploration into Science, Technology and Math that offer many paths for students and cultures to be expressed, allowing for a wide range of ideas flowing by. Meanwhile, some other schools, although don’t give the same level of opportunities as New Exploration into Science, Technology and Math, still give a chance of socializing that allows for ideas to spread. Each student has their own background and skills to bring to the table, allowing their experience to be viewed by many. Another case of these communities is with the trains, where you can see art from these communities, well depending on where you are. The trains are the main connection between each borough, so for all you know, there can be five people in the same train, yet live in each of the five boroughs. This means that these trains really do give the full experience of New York City.

There are many iconic parts of New York City like the Statue of Liberty, the Empire State Building, Central Park, the Bronx Zoo, the Brooklyn Bridge, and more. However, the place I most associate with New York City is the previously mentioned school, New Exploration into Science, Technology and Math (NEST+m). This school really is the perfect place to bring character and give the feeling of New York. With it being a rare kind of school that houses students from the young kindergarteners all the way to twelfth grade seniors. Its variety of clubs and mini celebrations that help cultures flow allows this school to really give the all in one bundle for what is New York City. Many of the events are worked upon by students like the Hindu holiday known as Holi, so different things happen to represent the month like Black History Month or Women History Month. The clubs are almost entirely done by students, so they really are the input for each thing. Since no two people think alike, it really allows for a lot of personalities to show from the many students. Giving this sense of community.

Overall, New York City is a place with a lot of variety of things and people in it. New York City has a unique environment for everyone. Each person has their own experience here. With each experience allowing for relations between people. Each correlation in the different experiences of people in New York gives the feeling of relatability and connections that bring people together. This is one of the main reasons why New York City stands out among other cities. New York City really is only complete with these bonds that travel through it like how the metro system transports people across the five boroughs. At the end of the day, there is something in New York City for each person to experience.
